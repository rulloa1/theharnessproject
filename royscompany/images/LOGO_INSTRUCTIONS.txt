LOGO PLACEHOLDER - INSTRUCTIONS
================================

Please add your company logo here as: logo.png

LOGO SPECIFICATIONS:
-------------------
- File name: logo.png
- Recommended size: 200x200 pixels minimum (can be larger)
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) or horizontal (2:1) works best
- Color: Should work well on dark backgrounds (the site uses a dark theme)
- Quality: High resolution for retina displays (2x or 3x recommended)

USAGE:
------
The logo appears in:
1. Navigation bar (top of every page) - 50px height
2. Footer - 40px height
3. Mobile menu sidebar - 40px height

TIPS:
-----
- Use a transparent background (PNG)
- If your logo has text, ensure it's readable at small sizes
- The logo should look good in both light and dark contexts
- Consider creating a version optimized for dark backgrounds
- Test the logo at different screen sizes

TEMPORARY PLACEHOLDER:
----------------------
Until you add your logo, the site will show:
- Company name text: "Roy's Company"
- Red gradient text effect
- Professional typography

Once you add logo.png to this folder, it will automatically appear throughout the site!
